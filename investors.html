The Opportunity

Three reasons this exists now.

Why now.

Large language models have reached the capability threshold where they can reliably handle complex operational workflows — not just answer questions. Why this vertical.

The MCKS pranic healing community spans 120+ countries with 100,000+ certified practitioners. It is globally distributed, culturally unified, WhatsApp-dependent, and completely unserved by purpose-built software. We are 69–82% cheaper than the nearest comparable at every tier — and infinitely more relevant.

Why us.

The founder is a 20-year enterprise technology professional who also runs a pranic healing centre. He lived the problem for years before building the solution. He is the first customer of his own product. This is the most defensible founder-market fit in this category — globally.
