Enerqubix AI Private Limited is an Indian AI company building vertical SaaS products for professional communities that have been systematically underserved by general-purpose software. Our registered office is in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Our platforms are built for global markets from day one.

We identify communities where the operational burden is acute and universal, where existing software was never designed for their reality, and where AI can replace that burden entirely — not partially. Then we build precisely for them.

The Founder

Murugesh P — Founder & CEO

I have spent twenty years building software and managing technology programmes for some of the largest organisations in the world. TCS. Cognizant. Squareshift. I have delivered enterprise transformation programmes across financial services, healthcare, and manufacturing.

And then I ran a pranic healing centre.

I opened Pranic Wellness Centre in Perungudi, Chennai, because I believe in what pranic healing gives to people. I had seen it work. I wanted to create a space where it could happen consistently and with quality.

Within weeks, I discovered a problem that no amount of enterprise software experience had prepared me for — not because it was technically complex, but because no one had ever thought it was worth solving. The healers I worked with, and every centre head I met across India and globally, were spending two to three hours every day on administrative work. They were not healing. They were administrating.

And there was no software built for them. Every wellness management tool I evaluated had been designed for yoga studios or physiotherapy chains — not for the specific operational reality of a small pranic healing centre run by volunteer healers with no dedicated administrator.

So I built LifeChiAI.

But as I built it, I realised the problem was not specific to pranic healing. It was structural. Across dozens of professional communities, the same pattern repeats: highly trained professionals, spending their most valuable hours on work that requires no training at all. That is the gap Enerqubix AI was built to close.

— Murugesh P Founder & CEO, Enerqubix AI Private Limited murugesh@enerqubix.ai
Purpose

Mission, Vision & Foundation

Mission

To give every professional community purpose-built AI that absorbs their operational burden — so they can practise their craft without interruption.

Vision

A world where the most skilled professionals in every field spend zero time on work that requires none of their skill.

Foundation

Enerqubix AI is a founder-led company. Product decisions come from living inside the problem. Privacy is architecture. Global from day one.

Values

How we build.

Precision Over Breadth

We build deeply for one community at a time. We would rather be indispensable to 10,000 practitioners than adequate for 10 million.

Built From the Inside

Every Enerqubix product begins with the founder living the problem. Not researching it. Not surveying it. Living it. This is the only way to build software that actually fits.

Privacy as Architecture

Patient data, practitioner data, financial data — all of it is sensitive. We design privacy into the data architecture before we write the first API route.

Global from Day One

India is our beachhead. Our pricing, payment infrastructure, and platform architecture are global from the first deployment.

Founder-Led Always

Product decisions, strategic direction, and investor relationships are led by the founder. This is not a constraint — it is the reason our products are as precise as they are.
