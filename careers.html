How we work.

Small Team · Full Ownership

Every engineer owns their domain end to end. There are no handoff boundaries between design, development, and deployment. You see customer impact directly.

Real AI Work

Not wrapper apps. Not chatbots. Operational AI agents that run production systems for real businesses. Claude Sonnet, Whisper, WhatsApp Business API — working together in a live product.

The team works remotely. We meet in Chennai quarterly. If you are in Chennai, you are welcome to work alongside the founder.
